Australian Charities:

16. Australia for UNHCR: Supports refugees globally.

17. The McGrath Foundation: Provides support for Australians affected by breast cancer.

18. The Smith Family: Offers educational support to disadvantaged Australian children.

19. Still Aware: Raises awareness of stillbirth and supports affected families.

20. Tour de Cure: Uses cycling events to fund cancer research.

21. Wheelchairs for Kids: Provides custom wheelchairs for children in need globally.

22. The Endeavour Foundation: Offers services for people with intellectual disabilities.

23. Diabetes NSW & ACT: Advocates and supports people living with diabetes.

24. Orange Sky Australia: Provides free laundry services to people experiencing homelessness.

25. Oxfam Australia: Works globally to combat poverty and injustice.

26. Population Services International: Focuses on global health issues like family planning and HIV.

27. One Acre Fund: Helps smallholder farmers in Africa to increase productivity and income.

28. Pathfinders: Delivers community services and support in rural and remote Australia.

29. Cancer Council Australia: Works on cancer research, support, and prevention.

30. Bush Heritage Australia: Focuses on conservation of Australia’s unique landscapes and wildlife.

2. CARE: Aims at fighting global poverty and social justice.

3. Americares: Provides crisis response and global health support.

4. International Rescue Committee (IRC): Offers aid to refugees and communities affected by conflict.

15. New Incentives: Encourages vaccination through conditional cash transfers.
