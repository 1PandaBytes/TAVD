Welcome to TAVD!

Here at The Australian Volunteering Directory(TAVD) our mission is to provide everything you need to become a successful volunteer for life. We have included information on how your skills can benefit the wider community, overseas and online opportunities, how you can found your own volunteering enterprise, charities that are worth putting money into and how to make time to volunteer.

Here we focus more on sparking ideas and innovation. If you would like some statistics on the matter feel free to check out the PDF attached from Volunteering Australia containing all the numbers your heart desires.
